Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Garlic, salt, pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic, add minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
